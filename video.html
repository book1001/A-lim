is a graphic designer based out of Seoul, South Korea.
Intrigued by both print and digital,

i.Am Type Specimen

Barcode Band ― The fourth project
Barcode Band was started in 2012 and has been broadcast in various media, including TV and Design Boom, selected as Vimeo Staff Pick and won ADAA. When the barcode graphics on the paper are scanned, they represent the inherent sounds of the graphics used to create music. This new digital instrument compresses existing heavy musical instruments onto a single piece of paper, exemplifying the essence of the digital society.

View original work: https://vimeo.com/41132461 — with Woonjin Kang and Yongduk Kim

Time Museum Visual Identity
The visual motif for this logo is a sundial and a mechanical clock, representing the first man-made clock juxtaposed against the clock in common use today.
The yellow hand located at the center of the logo was designed to rotate through 360 degrees, forming the shape of the sun.
